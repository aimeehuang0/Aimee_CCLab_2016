    Introduction

    The Health Care Industry is one of the biggest industries not only here in the Philippines but also globally. That's why in order to help in improving the quality and speed of the health care industry, there are a lot of new and innovative hospital information technology trending worldwide. There is a study for the Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services (CMS) that has been made by Mathematica Policy Research, Inc . (2006). It focuses on how the quality improvements within the hospital is being influenced by the information technology (IT) used. As part of the study, they have interviewed executives and they have been told that “IT has improved health care” in terms of speed, reduction of errors, and communication within the company.

  • Methodology: Agile

    The project team has chosen the Agile Methodology for it contains highly iterative development in providing quality services. Since customers (in this case, the patients) are given the highest priority first, continuous feedback are given importance for the reason that there can be frequent changes in the needs of the hospital as time passes by. And this methodology is used In order to come up with a system that fits the hospital's needs.
